Electronic Lab Management (ELM) Notebooks:

ELM Notebooks was a group capstone project for for CS464 Principals of Human-Computer Interaction course at CSU.

My group used a contextual inquiry approach to conduct observations/interviews with laboratory biologists in a real lab setting. We then applied user-centered design techniques to prototype an application meant to improve their workflow by eliminating breakdowns which were identified in the observation/interview stage.
